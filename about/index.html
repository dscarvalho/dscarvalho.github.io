About

Danilo Carvalho is an Assistant Professor at the Computer Science Department at the Federal University of Rio de Janeiro (UFRJ). Currently a postdoctoral research fellow at the Center for Technological Development in Health of Oswaldo Cruz Foundation (FIOCRUZ), working on Artificial Intelligence (AI) applied to innovation in healthcare. Also doing postdoctoral research at the Federal University of Rio de Janeiro (UFRJ) on the topic of Explainable AI applied to interlingual Natural Language Processing.

He holds a Ph.D. on Information Science from Japan Advanced Institute of Science and Technology (JAIST), having worked as a systems analyst within Brazilian state oil company (Petrobras) on job safety analysis (JSA) and environmental licensing control systems, followed by several scientific and technological projects in the fields of Artificial Intelligence, Computational Linguistics and Information Systems, such as Graphia (semantic relation extraction), EasyESA and TDV (semantic representation), R2NC (legal QA), among others.
